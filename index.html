Conditions We Treat

Comprehensive Pediatric GI Care

We treat a wide range of digestive, liver, and nutritional conditions in children from newborns to young adults.

Formula & Feeding Issues

Guidance for infants with formula intolerances, feeding difficulties, and nutritional concerns.

Abdominal Pain

Evaluation and management of chronic or recurrent abdominal pain in children of all ages.

Acid Reflux (GERD)

Diagnosis and treatment of gastroesophageal reflux disease and related symptoms.

Crohn's Disease & IBD

Specialized care for Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis, and inflammatory bowel conditions.

Liver Disease

Management of pediatric liver conditions including fatty liver, hepatitis, and enzyme elevation.

GI Bleeding

Expert evaluation and treatment of gastrointestinal bleeding in pediatric patients.

Bowel Disorders

Treating constipation, diarrhea, and other bowel-related issues in children.

Nutritional Disorders

Assessment and management of nutritional deficiencies, failure to thrive, and growth issues.
