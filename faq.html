Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about appointments, billing, prescriptions, and more.

Appointments
How long will my appointment take?

New patient appointments typically take 30–45 minutes. Follow-up visits are usually 15–30 minutes.

How early should I arrive before my appointment?

Please arrive 15 minutes early to verify your registration information and update any forms.

What is the wait time for a new patient appointment?

We are currently accepting new patients for all doctors. Urgent requests are typically seen within 2–5 business days of the request.

Are online appointments available?

Not at this time. Please call our office to schedule an appointment.

What are your hours of operation?

Monday–Friday: 8:30 AM – 12:00 PM & 1:00 PM – 4:30 PM. Closed on major holidays including New Year's Day, Labor Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas.

Can I see a nurse for a new patient appointment?

All new patients must see one of our Doctors first. Nurse Practitioners see patients for follow-up appointments only.

Billing & Insurance
Can I be seen without insurance?

Yes. We have self-pay options available. Please contact our office for specifics.

Do I pay my deductible at the time of the visit?

No. We will pend patient responsibility until we receive written confirmation from your insurance. Payment plans are available if needed.

What if I can't pay my balance in full?

Call (813) 870-4438, option 2 for billing to arrange a payment plan.

Who do I call for billing or insurance questions?

Call our main office at (813) 554-8986 for any billing, insurance, or general questions.

Prescriptions & Records
How do I get prescription refills?

Have the pharmacy contact our office directly. We refill within 24–48 hours if you have had a recent appointment.

Can I get lab or X-ray results over the phone?

No. Results are reviewed at a follow-up appointment. You'll be called if an earlier visit is needed.

How do I get copies of my medical records?

Contact our office. Personal copies: $1.00/page (first 25), then $0.25/page. Sent to another doctor's office: free. Allow 5–7 business days.

Is there someone in the office who speaks Spanish?

Yes! Dr. Cuenca speaks fluent Spanish, as do our Medical Assistant and one of our front desk clerks.

Who do I contact for an unresolved issue?

Contact the Office Manager at (813) 554-8617.
