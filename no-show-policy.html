No-Show Policy

We kindly ask for 24-hour notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your child's appointment.

To Our Parents:

A message from the management of Pediatric Gastroenterology & Nutrition of Tampa Bay

If you have an appointment with our office and are unable to attend, you must give our office 24-hour notice so that we may give the appointment to another child in need. In the event that 24-hour notice cannot be given, please give our office a courtesy call as soon as possible.

If we do not receive a call to cancel or reschedule your appointment, you will be responsible for a no-show fee. After the third missed appointment without notification, we will no longer be able to offer medical care for your child.

$25
No-Show Fee

Missed appointment without 24-hour cancellation notice.

$50
Procedure No-Show Fee

Missed scheduled procedure without 24-hour cancellation notice.

These fees are not covered by your insurance and must be paid before your next visit. After three missed appointments without notification, we will no longer be able to offer medical care for your child.

How to Cancel or Reschedule

1
Call at Least 24 Hours in Advance

Contact our office at (813) 870-4438 as early as possible before your scheduled appointment.

2
Leave a Message After Hours

If calling outside office hours, leave a clear voicemail with your name, child's name, and appointment date/time.

3
Reschedule as Soon as Possible

We understand emergencies happen. Call us to reschedule and we will do our best to accommodate your child's needs.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

– Management, Pediatric Gastroenterology & Nutrition of Tampa Bay

Need to Cancel or Reschedule?

Call us as soon as possible so we can accommodate another child in need and get you rescheduled quickly.
